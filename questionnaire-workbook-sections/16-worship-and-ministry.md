# Worship And Ministry

### Q102. Should corporate worship be governed only by what Scripture positively warrants?
- Category: Worship and ministry
- Answer: [ ] Yes  [ ] No
- Why this matters: This is the classic regulative-versus-normative principle question for public worship.
- Yes: `Regulative principle of worship`
- Yes proof texts: Deuteronomy 12:32; Leviticus 10:1-3; John 4:24; Colossians 2:23
- No: `Normative principle with broader freedom`
- No proof texts: Psalm 150; 1 Corinthians 9:22; 1 Corinthians 14:26, 40
- Note: This asks whether corporate worship should include only what Scripture positively warrants, not whether prudence is needed in applying biblical commands.
