# Closing Instructions

After completing the workbook:

1. Mark the questions you answered with the greatest confidence.

2. Mark the questions that still require study.

3. Review the questions where your answers create tension across categories and resolve those tensions where possible.

4. Group your answers by doctrinal locus.

5. Use the companion build guide to turn your answers into a written personal theology statement.

6. In the final document, prefer your strongest 2-6 supporting texts for each paragraph rather than reproducing every reference from the workbook.

The goal is not merely to hold opinions, but to be able to say with clarity: this is what I believe, this is why I believe it, and these are the texts that most directly ground my conclusions.

If the final document can be printed, handed to another Christian, and read as a clear account of your present doctrinal convictions, the workbook has done its job.
