# Spiritual Gifts

### Q97. Should private prayer in tongues be encouraged for personal edification?

- Answer: [ ] Yes  [ ] No

### Q98. Should all congregational prophecy be publicly weighed rather than automatically treated as the word of the Lord?
- Category: Spiritual gifts
- Answer: [ ] Yes  [ ] No
- Why this matters: This shapes the authority of prophetic speech and the church's duty of discernment.
- Yes: `Prophecy must be publicly weighed`
- Yes proof texts: 1 Corinthians 14:29; 1 Thessalonians 5:20-21
- No: `Prophecy ordinarily received as the word of the Lord`
- No proof texts: Deuteronomy 18:20-22; Jeremiah 23:28-29
- Note: The question is whether congregational prophecy should be tested as potentially mixed, not whether God can truly reveal.

### Q99. Is divine healing in the atonement expected ordinarily in the present age?
- Category: Spiritual gifts
- Answer: [ ] Yes  [ ] No
- Why this matters: This affects expectations for healing ministry and the already-not-yet relation of redemption to bodily restoration.
- Yes: `Ordinary present expectation of healing in the atonement`
- Yes proof texts: Isaiah 53:4-5; Matthew 8:16-17; James 5:14-16
- No: `Healing secured ultimately, not ordinarily guaranteed now`
- No proof texts: 2 Corinthians 12:7-10; Philippians 2:26-27; 1 Timothy 5:23
- Note: This concerns ordinary expectation in the present age, not whether God can and does heal in answer to prayer.
